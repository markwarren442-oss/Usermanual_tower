Phase 1 Summary

Base / Frame Assembly

Connections in this phase identify the points where the vertical layers connect. Match identical pegs and slots to stack each layer securely.
  • Align vertical connecting pegs on the underside of Layer 3 with the corresponding holes on the top rim of Layer 2. Press downward firmly until flush.
  • Repeat by aligning Layer 2 onto Layer 1, ensuring all corners are fully seated and level.

Phase 3: Final Structure

Vertical Assembly
In this final phase, assemble the tower vertically by stacking each section. Match the numerical codes (1, 2, 3, 4) at each connection point to ensure proper alignment and structural integrity.
  • Step-by-Step: Align the connector labeled with the corresponding number (e.g., 1 to 1) on the upper component with the connection point on the base.
  • Press firmly until flush before proceeding to the next section.
